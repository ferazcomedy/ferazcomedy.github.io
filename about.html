About FERAZ

Feraz is a comedian / performer based in New York City. His passion for comedy surfaced when he did an impression of President Obama auditioning for the role of Luke Skywalker in Star Wars.

He has been performing for audiences across Canada and the U.S. at comedy clubs and corporate events and won the "2016 Funniest Person in Finance" contest at Gotham Comedy Club in New York.
